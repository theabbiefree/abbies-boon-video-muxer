How it works: Your video and audio are played back simultaneously in real time while MediaRecorder captures both streams into a single muxed WebM file. No external libraries, no CDN, no upload — works everywhere.

Output: A muxed WebM containing your video + audio together. Drag it into CapCut or any editor to export as MP4, or post directly to platforms that accept WebM.

Duration: The mux runs for the length of your audio track in real time. A 3 minute track takes 3 minutes to mux.
